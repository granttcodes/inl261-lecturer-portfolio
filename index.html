About Me

I'm Ikraam Sadek, a lecturer in programming and software-related subjects across first and second year levels. I hold an Advanced Diploma in Application Development and take a practical, student-focused approach to teaching. I focus on engaging students through discussion, keeping them on track, and making sure no one falls behind. I'm also known for being approachable and always willing to help, especially when it comes to technical challenges.

Projects

Teaching & Module Development
I have been involved in teaching a wide range of programming and IT-related modules across both first and second year levels, helping students build strong foundational and practical skills.

Student Support & Engagement
I actively support students through discussions, check-ins, and hands-on guidance to ensure consistent progress and understanding.

Moodle & Technical Support
I am widely recognised for assisting both students and colleagues with Moodle-related challenges, often serving as a go-to person for technical support on campus.
